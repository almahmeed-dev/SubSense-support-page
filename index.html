Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new subscription?

Tap the + button in the top right corner of the main screen, then fill in the subscription details.

Can I set reminders for upcoming payments?

Yes! When adding or editing a subscription, enable reminders and choose how many days before the payment you'd like to be notified.

How do I export my subscription data?

Go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data, then choose your preferred format (JSON or CSV).

Troubleshooting

App is not syncing properly

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Force close and reopen the app
  3. Sign out and sign back in
  4. If issues persist, contact support
