Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SubSense ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our iOS app.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: SubSense does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All user information (such as subscriptions, settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device.
  • Payment Information: All purchases and subscriptions are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not access or store your payment details.
  • Analytics: SubSense does not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app functionality.
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions via the App Store.
  • To store your preferences and app data locally on your device.

Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or transfer your information to third parties. Your data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Children’s Privacy

SubSense does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove the information.
